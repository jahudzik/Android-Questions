What attributes are common for all widgets (inherited from View class)?
1 android:padding*
2 android:layout_margin*
3 android:visibility
4 android:nextFocus*
5 android:contentDescription

What's difference between View.getParent() and View.getRootView()?
- View.getParent() - gets the parent of this view
- View.getRootView() - gets the root of the tree
